Detailed Explanation of Technology

Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes. In modern times, technology plays a vital role in education, communication, healthcare, transportation, and space research.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence is a technology that enables machines to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. Examples include voice assistants, chatbots, and smart recommendation systems.

Robotics

Robotics involves designing machines that can perform tasks automatically. Robots are used in factories, hospitals, space missions, and disaster management.

Space Technology

Space technology helps in satellite communication, weather forecasting, navigation systems, and space exploration missions like Chandrayaan and Mars Orbiter.

Information Technology

Information Technology deals with computers, software, networks, and the internet. It has transformed education, banking, and global communication.
